About Us

Tšhwelepele Org is a community-driven nonprofit founded to uplift rural communities through empowerment, advocacy, and sustainable development. We stand for hope, dignity, and opportunity—aiming to spark a future where every household has at least one breadwinner.

Our work includes school uniform drives, youth entrepreneurship initiatives, and long-term goals such as building a community center with a clinic, a library, and a feeding scheme.

We are the voice for the unheard and the spark for a generation ready to thrive.
